- **QMN/MindQuest brand fit summary:** Moderate-to-strong fit. School anxiety is an emotional regulation challenge — fear of the unknown, separation anxiety, social newness. ## Market Gap Hypothesis

*Pre-filled for Elise to validate or reject through research.*

**Hypothesis:** The first-day-of-school niche is crowded with books that either:
1. Show the child having a perfect, happy first day (unrealistic)
2. Focus on the teacher being kind and the classroom being fun

A QMN angle would be different: focusing on the child's internal emotional experience — the fear of the unknown — and showing that courage is a small step they take even when scared, not a magical happy transformation. The school itself is less important than the emotional journey.

**Caution:** This is a highly competitive niche. Elise should assess whether the competition is beatable or whether this niche requires a more specific sub-angle to differentiate.

**Validation required:** Do reviews mention children who *still* struggled after reading most books? Do parents want more emotional depth, not just cheerful reassurance?
