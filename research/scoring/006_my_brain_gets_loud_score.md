## Market Gap Hypothesis

**Primary gap:** No children's picture book uses "loud brain" or overwhelm framing for ages 4–7. The search space is completely unclaimed.

**Secondary gap:** The existing SEL/anger management niche is dominated by workbooks and activity-based formats. A genuine narrative storybook is underserved.

**Tertiary gap:** Most anger/big feelings books enter at the peak of the emotion. The working "loud brain" positioning enters upstream — at the overwhelm signal before the meltdown — giving parents and children an earlier intervention language. No competitor addresses this pre-anger overwhelm moment.

**Validation still needed:** Elise should also search: `loud brain children's book`, `overwhelm picture book kids 4-7`, `my brain is loud kids`. These related phrases would confirm the gap holds across multiple entry points.

## Copyright / IP Risk Notes

*See `research/ip_watchlist.md` for full entries on each flag below.*

**⚠ Inside Out (Disney/Pixar) — MEDIUM risk** (Watchlist entry #1 — Open/Medium)
Both Inside Out films feature emotions as characters living inside a child's head in a "brain control room." This is the primary IP risk for any children's book that personifies emotions or the brain.

**How to steer clear:** Child is the protagonist — not personified brain characters. Brain is a sensation the child experiences, not a character or setting. No control-room or headquarters visual. No Inside Out color-coding. Document these guardrails in every illustration brief after Gate 8 clears title/IP/SEO.

**Other IP notes:**
- The Awkward Yeti's "Heart and Brain" comic series: adult-only audience, no children's book trademark exposure. QMN brain character must be clearly original and visually distinct from the Awkward Yeti aesthetic.
- "Loud Brain" as a character name or title phrase: no existing trademark found in current research notes. Elise must conduct USPTO/title search before finalizing any character name or title.
- "The Little Loud Brain" and "My Brain Gets Really Loud" are working title directions only. Neither is approved as the final title.

**Overall IP risk level:** [ ] Low  [x] **Medium** — Inside Out proximity requires deliberate art direction; manageable with clear execution guidelines after Gate 8 title/IP/SEO checks.

**Disqualifying?** No — IP Safety score 7/10 is well above the disqualifying threshold of ≤ 3.
