## Rough gap hypothesis  (optional — the command will refine it)

gap: Enter the validated big-feelings market through a differentiated emotional doorway — the brain getting "loud"/overwhelmed BEFORE anger — rather than another "control your anger" workbook or mascot. Treat anger as information, not a problem to suppress. Story-first for 4-7 with one light calming tool. Avoid: dragon/spot/blob metaphors and any Inside Out-style emotion characters.
