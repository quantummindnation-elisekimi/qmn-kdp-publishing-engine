## Review Strategy (Amazon-Compliant Only)

**Compliant methods:**
- Include a brief, honest review request in the back matter of the book: "If [Wren]'s story helped your child feel a little braver, your review means the world to us. Thank you."
- Organic discovery — parents who buy the book and find it helpful will leave reviews naturally
- Personal network outreach — friends and family who purchase and read the book (must actually read it; must not be coerced)

**Not permitted:**
- Paid reviews
- Review exchange programs
- Offering discounts or bonuses in exchange for reviews
- Reviewing your own book
- Using multiple accounts to inflate ratings

*Amazon takes review manipulation seriously. Violations risk account suspension.*

## 30-Day Post-Launch Review Plan

At 30 days post-publication, Elise reviews KDP analytics and notes:

**What to check:**
- Units sold (print and eBook separately)
- KDP page reads (if in KU)
- BSR range observed (screenshot or note)
- Review count and average rating
- Any keyword impressions data (KDP Advertising console if applicable)
- Any listing quality flags from Amazon

**What triggers optimization:**
- If units sold in 30 days is zero with no reviews → review keyword strategy and description
- If BSR is not in the top 100,000 in any category → review category selection
- If page visits are decent but conversion is low → review description and cover

**All changes require Elise review before updating the live listing.**
