## Typography Plan (Draft)

*Elise selects final fonts. These are suggestions only.*

**Heading font (title page, section headers):**
- Suggestion: A rounded, friendly sans-serif or soft display font — nothing sharp or aggressive
- Size: 36–48pt for the title page; 28–32pt for section openers

**Body/story text font:**
- Suggestion: A clean, highly legible rounded sans-serif or soft serif — readable at 14pt for ages 4–7 read-aloud use
- Size: 14–18pt (minimum 14pt for this age range)
- Line spacing: 1.4–1.6x for readability
- Alignment: Left-aligned or centered — test both at read-aloud pace

**Text placement on standard spreads:**
- Story text sits in the lower third of the spread (below or alongside the illustration)
- Text box has consistent padding from illustration area — minimum 0.25" gap
- Text does not overlap illustration on standard spreads

*Note: Specific Canva font choices must be confirmed as part of QMN Brand Kit or approved individually by Elise.*

## Page Layout Structures

### Standard Story Spread (Most Pages)

- Full bleed illustration covering the spread
- Text box in lower third or lower quarter of the spread
- Text background: semi-transparent warm overlay OR clean separate white/cream text band
- Test: Is text readable against the illustration background?

### Emotional/Metaphorical Spread (Pages 7–8, 15–16)

- Illustration is more abstract/symbolic
- Text may be more centered on the page
- Allow more white space around text to give it breathing room

### Full-Bleed Illustration Spread (Pages 19–20 — the turning point)

- No text on the full illustration spread — illustration carries the emotional weight
- Text appears on the following page

### Activity Pages (Pages 33–36)

- Clean, simple layout with clear heading
- Large drawing/writing spaces for children
- Friendly font; instructions in 14–16pt
- Simple illustrations or decorative elements as page borders (all documented)

- Title: "The Little Brave Brain" — large, central or upper-center, high-contrast, warm golden or deep warm color
- Subtitle: "A Storybook for Kids Who Feel Scared to Try New Things" — smaller, below title, readable at thumbnail size

- Title (shortened if needed): Large enough to read

**Back Cover:**
- 2–3 sentence description (from approved book description)
- Age range: 4–7 years
- Short tagline or series line
- Barcode placeholder (lower right — KDP will place barcode)
- QMN branding

**Thumbnail test:** Export cover at 160px wide and check: Is the title readable? Does the character image read clearly?
