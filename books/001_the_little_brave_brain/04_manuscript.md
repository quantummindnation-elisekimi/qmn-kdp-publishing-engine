## Activity Pages (Optional — Pages 33–36)

### Activity Page 1: My Brave Step

**Title:** "My Brave Step"
**Instructions:** Draw a picture of something new you want to try. It's okay if it feels a little scary!
**Space for:** Full-page drawing

### Activity Page 2: What My Brain Says

**Title:** "What Does Your Brain Say?"
**Instructions:** Fill in the thought bubbles! What does your brain say when you're scared? What does your brave brain say back?
**Format:** Two thought bubble illustrations — one cool/nervous, one warm/brave

### Activity Page 3: My Brave Brain Journal

**Title:** "I Tried Something New!"
**Instructions:** Draw or write about a time you tried something new.
- What was the new thing?
- How did your tummy feel before?
- How did it feel after?

### Activity Page 4: For Grown-Ups

**Title:** "A Note for Grown-Ups"
**Content:** Brief parent guide — how to use the book with a cautious child, conversation starters, notes on validating fear while encouraging courage.
