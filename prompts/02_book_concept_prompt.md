# Prompt 02: Book Concept — QMN KDP Publishing Engine

*Use this prompt to develop the book brief after a niche has been approved.*

---

## When to Use This Prompt

After:
- Niche has been researched and scored
- Niche has been approved by Elise (Gate 3 passed)
- You are ready to develop the book concept and positioning

---

## Prompt (Copy and use with Claude)

```
I'm developing an original children's picture book for Amazon KDP under the QMN / MindQuest brand.

Approved niche: [NICHE THEME]
Niche score: [SCORE]/100
Target audience: [AGE RANGE]
Parent pain point: [SPECIFIC PAIN POINT — from research]

Please help me develop a book brief with:

1. A working title (original — not matching any existing Amazon title)
2. A working subtitle that speaks to the parent's search intent
3. A specific emotional arc for the child reader:
   - Opening emotion
   - Complicating emotion (middle)
   - Turning point
   - Closing emotion
   - Takeaway for the child
4. A unique angle that differentiates this book from the top 5 competitors in this niche
5. QMN/MindQuest brand fit — how does this connect to emotional intelligence, mindset, courage, or growth?
6. Series potential — suggest 2–3 possible follow-up titles in the same character world or theme family

IMPORTANT CONSTRAINTS:
- The title must be original. Do not suggest any title that matches or closely resembles an existing Amazon title.
- No references to any franchise characters, brands, studios, or trademarked IP.
- No artist style references.
- The emotional arc must be specific — not generic. It should reflect a real child experience.
- Do not guarantee sales, rankings, or Amazon approval.
- This is a draft concept only. Elise will review and approve before any character or manuscript work begins.
```
