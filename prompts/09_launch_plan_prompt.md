*Use this prompt to draft the launch plan before KDP submission.*

---

- Proceeding to Phase 13 (Launch Plan)

---

## Prompt (Copy and use with Claude)

```
I'm creating a launch plan for a children's picture book on Amazon KDP under the QMN / MindQuest brand.

BOOK DETAILS:
- Title: [TITLE]
- Audience: Ages [RANGE]
- Theme: [THEME]
- Target launch date (approximate): [DATE]
- Format: Print [ ] eBook [ ]
- KDP Select enrollment: Yes [ ] No [ ]
- QMN brand channels available: [e.g., email list, social media platform, MindQuest community]

Please help me draft a launch plan that includes:

1. PRE-LAUNCH CHECKLIST
   - Final validation tasks before clicking submit on KDP
   - Any community/audience preparation

2. KDP UPLOAD PLAN
   - Recommended upload sequence (eBook first vs. print first)
   - Expected KDP review timeline note

3. PRICING STRATEGY
   - Initial launch price recommendation for print
   - Initial launch price for eBook (if applicable)
   - Any promotional pricing considerations for launch week

4. REVIEW STRATEGY (Amazon-compliant only)
   - Policy-safe methods to build initial reviews
   - What to include in the book's back matter (honest review request)
   - Legitimate outreach approach

5. QMN/MINDQUEST PROMOTIONAL PLAN
   - How to introduce this book to the QMN audience
   - Social media approach
   - Connection to MindQuest brand positioning
   - Any companion content (printables, activity sheets) to offer

6. 30-DAY CHECK-IN PLAN
   - What to review in KDP analytics at 30 days
   - What metrics indicate the listing needs optimization
   - What metrics indicate the launch is working

IMPORTANT CONSTRAINTS:
- No paid review schemes, review swaps, or review manipulation — these violate Amazon policy.
- Do not promise or imply guaranteed sales, rankings, or revenue.
- All launch activity must be Amazon-compliant.
- This is a plan only. Elise reviews and approves before any launch activity begins.
- Do not suggest submitting to KDP before all approval gates are cleared.
```
