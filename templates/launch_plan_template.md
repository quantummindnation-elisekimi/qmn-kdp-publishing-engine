## Review Strategy

*Amazon reviews are critical for discoverability. All review acquisition must be 100% policy-compliant.*

**Compliant review methods:**
- Organic — buyers discover and review the book naturally
- Author page / back matter — include a brief, honest request to leave a review if the child enjoyed the book
- Personal network — share with friends/family who purchase and read the book (they must have actually read it)
- ARC (Advance Review Copy) — send to genuine readers before publication; disclose that a free copy was provided

**Prohibited:**
- Paid reviews
- Review swaps ("review mine and I'll review yours")
- Incentivized reviews (offering discounts, bonuses, or gifts in exchange for reviews)
- Reviewing your own book or using family accounts
- Any method that violates Amazon's Review Policies
