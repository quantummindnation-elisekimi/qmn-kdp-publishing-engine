# Book Quality Checklist — QMN KDP Publishing Engine

*This is the pre-publication quality standard for every book in this system.*

---

## Why Quality Matters

Amazon KDP is flooded with AI-generated filler content. QMN/MindQuest books must be visually, emotionally, and editorially superior to the average self-published children's book. This checklist ensures every book published through this system earns its place on a shelf — digital or physical.

A book that passes this checklist:
- Has been genuinely researched against real parent/child needs
- Has original characters not seen elsewhere
- Has been read aloud by a human adult
- Has illustrations or illustration plans that are original and IP-safe
- Has a KDP listing that serves the buyer's search intent
- Has had a copyright check completed and signed

A book that does not pass this checklist does not ship.

---

## Story Quality

- [ ] The story addresses a specific, real parent/child emotional need — not just a vague theme
- [ ] The story has a complete arc: problem → struggle → turning point → resolution
- [ ] The emotional journey is age-appropriate for the stated audience
- [ ] The language has been tested at read-aloud speed — it flows naturally
- [ ] Sentence length and vocabulary are appropriate for the target age
- [ ] The ending is satisfying and affirming — the child reading should feel something real
- [ ] Activity pages (if included) are genuinely useful and age-appropriate
- [ ] No filler pages — every page earns its place in the story

---

## Character Quality

- [ ] The main character has a clear, specific personality — not a generic "nice kid"
- [ ] The character's problem/fear/challenge is specific enough to feel real
- [ ] The character's journey is emotionally honest — not artificially easy
- [ ] Supporting characters serve the story — they are not decoration
- [ ] All character names have been searched for trademark risk
- [ ] No character resembles a known franchise character in name, appearance, or concept

---

## Illustration Quality (or Illustration Plan Quality)

- [ ] Every illustration or illustration brief describes a specific, original visual moment from the story
- [ ] No artist or studio style references appear anywhere in prompts or notes
- [ ] The illustration style is consistent throughout the book
- [ ] Illustrations support and extend the text — they do not just repeat it
- [ ] Cover illustration is original, high-quality, and readable as a thumbnail
- [ ] All illustration sources are documented with license information

---

## Canva and Design Quality

- [ ] Trim size is correct for KDP
- [ ] Bleed is applied correctly
- [ ] Text is within safe margins
- [ ] Font choices are appropriate for children's reading age
- [ ] Color palette is consistent and appropriate for the story's emotional tone
- [ ] QMN Brand Kit is applied correctly
- [ ] All Canva assets are documented in `06_canva_notes.md`
- [ ] Cover thumbnail reads clearly at approximately 160px width

---

## KDP Listing Quality

- [ ] Title is original and not an exact match of any existing Amazon title
- [ ] Subtitle contains genuine search-relevant language for the target parent
- [ ] Book description speaks to the parent's pain point before describing the book
- [ ] Book description makes no false claims or guaranteed outcomes
- [ ] Backend keywords are relevant and do not violate KDP keyword policies
- [ ] Categories are appropriate for the content and audience
- [ ] Age range and reading level are accurate
- [ ] AI disclosure has been completed if applicable

---

## Legal and Policy Quality

- [ ] `09_copyright_check.md` is complete and signed by Elise
- [ ] Character name search results are documented
- [ ] Title search results are documented
- [ ] AI content disclosure status is documented and completed on KDP
- [ ] Canva asset licensing is documented
- [ ] No trademarked characters, brands, or celebrities appear in the book
- [ ] No artist style references appear in illustration documentation

---

## Final Human Review

- [ ] Elise has personally read the full manuscript
- [ ] Elise has approved the character bible
- [ ] Elise has approved the illustration prompts
- [ ] Elise has signed the copyright check
- [ ] Elise has approved the KDP metadata
- [ ] Elise has approved the final PDFs
- [ ] Elise has signed the final publish checklist (`10_final_checklist.md`)

---

## The Anti-Spam Test

Before submitting, answer honestly:

1. Would a parent genuinely choose this book over the top 3 existing Amazon results?
2. Does this book do something those books do not?
3. Would you be comfortable with your name on this book?

If the answer to any of these is "no" or "not sure" — do not publish yet. Improve the book first.
