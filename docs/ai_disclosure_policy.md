# AI Disclosure Policy — QMN KDP Publishing Engine

*Amazon KDP currently requires disclosure of AI-generated content. This document explains what that means for this project and how to handle it correctly.*

---

## Why AI Disclosure Matters

Amazon's content policies require publishers to disclose the use of AI-generated content when publishing on KDP. Failure to disclose when required can result in:
- Listing removal
- Account suspension or termination
- Future publishing restrictions

This policy exists to keep QMN's KDP account in good standing and to be honest with readers.

---

## What Counts as AI-Generated Content (Per KDP Policy)

Amazon KDP currently distinguishes between:

**AI-generated content** (requires disclosure):
- Text written entirely by an AI tool with minimal human editing
- Images created by an AI image generator (Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, Adobe Firefly, etc.)
- Translations produced entirely by AI

**AI-assisted content** (may not require disclosure, but document it internally):
- Text originally written by a human, then edited or refined with AI suggestions
- Research organized or structured by AI from human-provided inputs
- Metadata/descriptions written by humans with AI-suggested keywords

*Note: Amazon's specific requirements may change. Always check the current KDP Content Policy at the time of each submission. This document reflects policy understanding as of mid-2026 and must be verified before each book submission.*

## How to Complete AI Disclosure on KDP

When uploading to Amazon KDP:

1. In the KDP content upload dashboard, look for the AI-generated content disclosure section
2. Answer honestly based on the content type:
   - **Text content**: If significant AI generation was used in the final manuscript, select "Yes, this book contains AI-generated content"
   - **Images/Illustrations**: If any illustration was generated by an AI tool, select "Yes, this book contains AI-generated images"
   - **Translation**: N/A for this project
3. Document the disclosure status in `09_copyright_check.md` under "AI Disclosure Status"

---

## Internal Documentation Requirements

For every book, maintain a record in `09_copyright_check.md` of:
- Which tools were used in production (Claude, Midjourney, DALL-E, etc.)
- What each tool produced
- How much human editing was applied
- What AI disclosure selection was made on KDP
- Date of KDP submission

## Important Reminders

- AI disclosure requirements may change. Verify the current KDP policy at every submission.
- Do not underestimate how much AI was used. Honest disclosure protects the account.
- Being AI-assisted does not make a book bad. QMN's competitive advantage is the quality of human direction, curation, and judgment — not hiding AI involvement.
- Disclosing AI use is not a competitive disadvantage. Many KDP publishers use AI. The quality of the book is what differentiates.
